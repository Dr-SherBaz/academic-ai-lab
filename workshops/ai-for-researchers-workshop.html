AI Tools for Academic Researchers: Practical Workshop

May 4, 2026 1 min read

A hands-on workshop covering AI tools for literature review, data analysis, academic writing, and research productivity. Suitable for all disciplines.

  • What You Will Learn

Workshop Overview

This practical workshop introduces researchers to AI tools that can accelerate

literature review, enhance data analysis, improve academic writing, and

streamline research workflows — all while maintaining academic integrity.

What You Will Learn

Session 1: AI-Assisted Literature Review

  • Using AI search tools to find relevant papers faster
  • Summarising and synthesising research articles
  • Identifying research gaps with AI assistance
  • Managing citations and bibliographies efficiently

Session 2: AI for Data Analysis

  • AI-assisted data cleaning and preparation
  • Natural language queries for statistical exploration
  • Generating and interpreting descriptive statistics
  • Visualising data with AI-powered tools

Session 3: AI-Enhanced Academic Writing

  • Improving clarity and academic tone
  • Paraphrasing and avoiding unintentional plagiarism
  • Structuring arguments with AI feedback
  • Ethical boundaries: what AI should and should not do

Session 4: Research Productivity Workflow

  • Building a personal AI-assisted research pipeline
  • Integrating AI tools into your existing workflow
  • Best practices for documenting AI use in research
  • Institutional policies and ethical considerations

Who Should Attend

This workshop is designed for:

  • PhD candidates at any stage
  • Early-career researchers
  • Faculty members exploring AI in research
  • Research supervisors who want to guide students effectively
  • Graduate students preparing for thesis work

Format

  • 4-hour online session with breaks
  • Live demonstrations
  • Hands-on exercises
  • Q&A throughout
  • Resource pack provided after the session

Prerequisites

  • Basic familiarity with academic research processes
  • A laptop or desktop computer
  • No prior AI experience required

Registration

Spaces are limited to ensure individual attention. Register your interest

and we will confirm your place with session details and materials.
